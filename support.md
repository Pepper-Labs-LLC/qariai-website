## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**Q: How do I start using Qari AI?**  
A: Simply open the app, select a Sheikh's recitation to practice with, and start recording your recitation. The AI will analyze your performance in real-time and provide feedback on pitch, timing, and pronunciation.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**  
A: No, Qari AI does not require you to create an account. You can start using the app immediately after installation.

**Q: Is my audio data stored?**  
A: No, your audio recordings are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers. They are deleted immediately after analysis is complete.

### Subscriptions and Premium Features

**Q: How do I subscribe to premium features?**  
A: Premium features can be accessed through in-app purchases. Follow the prompts in the app to subscribe.

**Q: How do I manage or cancel my subscription?**  
A: Subscriptions are managed through Apple. You can view, modify, or cancel your subscription by going to:
- Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions
- Or visit [Apple's Subscription Management page](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202039)

**Q: I was charged but don't have access to premium features.**  
A: Please ensure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase. If issues persist, contact us at pepperlabs8@gmail.com with your Apple ID email and purchase receipt.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Refund requests are handled by Apple. You can request a refund through [Apple's Report a Problem page](https://reportaproblem.apple.com/).

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app crashes or freezes.**  
A: Try the following troubleshooting steps:
1. Force close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your device
3. Ensure you have the latest version of the app installed
4. Check that your device meets the minimum iOS requirements

If the issue persists, contact us with details about when the crash occurs and your device model.

**Q: The AI analysis isn't working.**  
A: Please check:
- Your internet connection is active
- You have granted microphone permissions to the app
- You're speaking clearly into the microphone
- The app is up to date

**Q: I'm having audio recording issues.**  
A: Ensure that:
- Microphone permissions are enabled in Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone
- No other apps are using the microphone
- Your device's microphone is not blocked or damaged

**Q: The app is slow or laggy.**  
A: Try:
- Closing other apps to free up device memory
- Restarting your device
- Ensuring you have a stable internet connection
- Updating to the latest version of the app

**Q: Can I practice specific verses or surahs?**
